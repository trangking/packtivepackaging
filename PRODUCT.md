# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

B2B buyers from factories and brands (cosmetics, food, consumer goods) who need custom packaging manufactured — OEM/ODM packaging orders. They land on the site while sourcing a packaging supplier and need to quickly judge capability, range, and credibility before reaching out.

## Product Purpose

Marketing site for Packtive Packaging, a packaging manufacturer. Primary goal is lead generation: get B2B visitors to request a quote or contact the company to start a production order. Success is measured by inquiries/contact submissions, not pageviews.

## Brand Personality

Premium & Modern — confident, polished, technically competent. Should read as a manufacturer with serious production capability and design sense, not a generic print shop. Reference: napat-packaging.com (clean grid layout, product-led imagery, prominent "request quote" CTAs, trust indicators like client logos, accordion FAQ) — match its B2B clarity and trust-building patterns, but elevate the visual polish toward "premium/modern" rather than purely utilitarian.

## Anti-references

None specified yet. Avoid generic stock-photo-heavy layouts and cluttered, print-shop-style pages that undercut the premium positioning.

## Design Principles

- Product and capability are the hero — let packaging photography carry the page, not decorative chrome.
- Every key page should funnel toward a quote/contact action; CTAs repeat rather than hide in one place.
- Build trust fast — credibility signals (clients, portfolio, FAQ) should appear before the visitor has to scroll far.
- Premium polish over budget/generic — typography, spacing, and imagery should look deliberately art-directed, not templated.
- Clarity for a B2B sourcing decision — straightforward language and structure over emotional storytelling.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No specific requirements stated yet; default to WCAG AA (contrast, focus states, readable type sizes) as a baseline for a public B2B marketing site.
